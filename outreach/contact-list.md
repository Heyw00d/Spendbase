## Email Pattern Cheat Sheet

Most crypto companies use:
- firstname@company.com
- firstname.lastname@company.com  
- hello@company.com
- team@company.com
- partnerships@company.com
- marketing@company.com
- press@company.com

Test with tools like Hunter.io or Clearbit Connect.
