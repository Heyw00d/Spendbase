Frequently Asked Questions

What is Coinbase?
Coinbase is a crypto-native crypto debit card that allows you to spend your cryptocurrency at merchants worldwide. It operates on the Visa network and supports Base blockchain.
How much cashback does Coinbase offer?
Coinbase offers 1-4% cashback rewards in BTC/ETH/DOGE.
Is Coinbase self-custody?
Coinbase is custodial, meaning the platform holds and manages your cryptocurrency.
Where can I use Coinbase?
Coinbase is available in americas and can be used anywhere Visa is accepted.
