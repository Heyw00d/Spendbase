Overview

The KuCoin Card, branded as KuCard, is a Visa debit card from KuCoin, one of the world's largest cryptocurrency exchanges. It allows users to spend their crypto holdings at any Visa-accepting merchant worldwide with instant crypto-to-fiat conversion at the point of sale.

KuCard offers tiered cashback rewards of up to 3%, based on the user's asset level on the exchange. Entry-level users with 1,000 EUR in assets qualify for 1.2% cashback (capped at €10/month), while higher tiers unlock better rates and limits. The card has no annual fee and supports Apple Pay and Google Pay integration.

The card is custodial — funds are held on the KuCoin exchange — and is primarily available in Europe and select global regions. It's best suited for existing KuCoin users who want a simple way to spend their exchange balances in everyday life.
