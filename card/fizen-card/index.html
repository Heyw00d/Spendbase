Overview

The Fizen Card is a Mastercard debit card from Fizen, a crypto neobank focused on stablecoin payments, yield, and global remittances. Unlike most crypto cards, Fizen maintains a fully non-custodial model with MPC (Multi-Party Computation) and biometric security.

Fizen allows users to pay with crypto worldwide using both QR codes and traditional card payments. The platform supports stablecoin-native spending, meaning users can hold and spend USDC and other stablecoins without pre-converting to fiat. Background yield features let users earn on idle crypto balances.

The card targets crypto users who prioritize self-custody and want a neobank-like experience without sacrificing control of their assets. With global remittance features and multi-chain support, Fizen positions itself as a bridge between DeFi and everyday banking.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Fizen truly non-custodial?
Yes, Fizen uses MPC (Multi-Party Computation) technology with biometric recovery, so users always maintain control of their assets.
What can I do with Fizen besides card payments?
Fizen offers stablecoin payments, background yield on idle crypto, global remittances, and QR-based payments in addition to the Mastercard.
What chains does Fizen support?
Fizen supports multiple chains for payments and remittances. Check fizen.io for the latest list of supported networks.
