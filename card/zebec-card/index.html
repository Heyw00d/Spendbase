Overview

The Zebec Card is a Mastercard debit card from Zebec, a multi-chain payment protocol supporting 18+ blockchains and over 150 tokens. The card uses a hybrid custody model — your funds remain in self-custody until you load them onto the card, at which point they're converted for spending.

Zebec offers three card tiers: Silver, Carbon, and Black. The Silver and Carbon tiers have no annual fee, while the Carbon tier stands out with zero transaction fees. The Black tier offers unlimited spending limits and premium perks with variable pricing. All tiers support Apple Pay and Google Pay integration.

The card can be reloaded up to $10,000 per day and supports spending in USD, EUR, and GBP. With global availability and broad multi-chain support, Zebec targets crypto-native users who want maximum chain flexibility without sacrificing the convenience of traditional card payments.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Zebec Card truly non-custodial?
Zebec uses a hybrid model — your funds stay in self-custody until you load them onto the card. Once loaded, they become custodial for spending purposes.
How many blockchains does Zebec support?
Zebec supports 18+ blockchains including Solana, Ethereum, BNB Chain, and many others, with over 150 tokens available.
What are the Zebec Card tiers?
Zebec offers three tiers: Silver (free), Carbon (free, zero transaction fees), and Black (premium with unlimited limits and variable pricing).
