Overview

The Jupiter Card is a Visa debit card launched in January 2026 by Jupiter, the dominant DEX aggregator on Solana. With over $200B in cumulative trading volume and one of the largest communities in crypto, Jupiter's move into payments is a significant step for the Solana ecosystem.

The card allows users to spend directly from their Jupiter account with real-time conversion of any supported token to fiat at the point of sale. There are no FX fees and no annual fee, making it one of the more competitive exchange-backed cards in the market.

Jupiter Card rewards users in JUP tokens, though the exact cashback rate is still being finalized. The card is custodial — funds are held by Jupiter — and operates on the Solana blockchain, giving it access to fast and cheap transactions. It's ideal for active Solana DeFi users who already route trades through Jupiter.
